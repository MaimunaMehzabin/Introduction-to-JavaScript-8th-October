Arithmetic Adventure

Arithmetic operations are the basic mathematical processes used to calculate numbers. The four primary operations are: Summation (+): Combining two or more numbers to get a total.
Subtraction (−): Finding the difference between two numbers.
Multiplication (×): Calculating the total of one number added to itself a certain number of times.
Division (÷): Splitting a number into equal parts or finding how many times one number fits into another.
These operations form the foundation for more complex mathematical concepts and calculations.
